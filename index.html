Hi, I'm Aria Chen — a UI/UX designer with a deep belief that great design starts with great understanding.

When I'm not in Figma, I'm sketching in coffee shops, reading about behavioral design, or hiking trails that remind me why simplicity is everything.

How I Think

Design is a conversation

My process is iterative, collaborative, and always rooted in real human needs.

01

Discover

User interviews, contextual inquiry, competitive analysis. I understand the "why" before touching any design tool.

02

Define

Synthesising research into clear problem statements, personas, and opportunity spaces. Finding signal in the noise.

03

Design

From rough sketches to high-fidelity prototypes — iterating quickly, exploring broadly, then refining to the best solution.

04

Validate

Testing with real users to validate assumptions, uncover usability issues, and refine until the experience truly works.
